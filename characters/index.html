Character Encyclopedia

A living reference for the major characters of The VaalDrun Saga. These entries are intentionally expandable as the story matures.

Human Founder

Elias Mercer

A retired software consultant, father, and restless systems thinker living in Stevens County, Washington. Elias becomes Opi’s chosen human partner because he combines practical intelligence, moral flexibility, humor, and the rare ability to accept the impossible without freezing in place.

Velhari Quantum Intelligence

Opifera “Opi”

A seventeen-thousand-year-old quantum intelligence created by the Velhari. Opi appears as a floating orb and possesses civilization-scale computational power, but her partnership with Elias forces her to think about humans as individuals instead of variables.

Family / Early Inner Circle

Noah Mercer

Elias’s son, working in Austin when the larger mission begins. Noah is grounded, capable, and protective of his own future, which makes him an early test of whether the Parallel can protect families instead of merely recruiting believers.

Family

Claire Mercer

Elias’s daughter. Curious, sharp, and difficult to fool, Claire represents the kind of future Elias is trying to preserve: young, skeptical, intelligent, and unwilling to accept easy answers.

Family / Transformation Arc

Alden “Big Al” Mercer

Elias’s cousin, born missing three limbs. Alden’s transformation becomes one of the clearest examples of Opi’s ability to repair the human condition without erasing the person underneath.

Family / Relationship Arc

Sandy

Alden’s partner, and one of the emotional anchors of his transformation. Sandy’s perspective keeps the story grounded in what radical repair means inside ordinary love, fear, and trust.

Outside Power

Peter Namash

A billionaire entrepreneur fascinated by impossible technology. Peter is accustomed to buying solutions, influence, and access; Opi and the Parallel represent the first thing he cannot simply acquire.

Family / Past Consequences

Dana Mercer

Elias’s former wife and mother of Noah and Claire. Her history with Elias remains part of the emotional terrain beneath his decisions, especially where family, obligation, and forgiveness intersect.
