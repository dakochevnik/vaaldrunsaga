Characters

Main Characters

Elias Mercer

Retired software consultant, problem solver, and reluctant architect of humanity's second chance.

Opifera “Opi”

A seventeen-thousand-year-old Velhari Quantum Intelligence charged with preventing the extinction of humanity.

Family

Noah Mercer

Elias' son. A Tesla production associate in Austin whose work ethic, intelligence, and steady character make him one of Opi's favorite humans.

Claire Mercer

Elias' daughter. Curious, highly intelligent, and relentlessly inquisitive. Claire possesses many of her father's strengths and more than a few of his habits.

Alden “Big Al” Mercer

Elias' cousin. Born missing three limbs, Big Al has spent a lifetime overcoming challenges with humor, stubbornness, and optimism.

Dana Mercer

Elias' former wife and the mother of Noah and Claire. Her choices years earlier continue to shape the lives of everyone around her.

Others

Peter Namash

A billionaire entrepreneur whose fascination with impossible technology gradually grows into obsession. Peter is used to buying solutions to every problem he encounters. Opi represents the first problem money may not be able to solve.
