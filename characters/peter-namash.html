Peter Namash

Outside Power

A billionaire entrepreneur fascinated by impossible technology. Peter is accustomed to buying solutions, influence, and access; Opi and the Parallel represent the first thing he cannot simply acquire.

Current Role

This entry is a starter encyclopedia page and should be expanded as the published chapters grow. It is designed for readers who want a quick, spoiler-light reference to the character and their place in the saga.

Story Function

Peter helps define one of the central questions of the series: what happens when damaged, brilliant, ordinary, or overlooked people are placed inside a civilization-scale rescue attempt with tools powerful enough to change human destiny?
