Dana Mercer

Family / Past Consequences

Elias’s former wife and mother of Noah and Claire. Her history with Elias remains part of the emotional terrain beneath his decisions, especially where family, obligation, and forgiveness intersect.

Current Role

This entry is a starter encyclopedia page and should be expanded as the published chapters grow. It is designed for readers who want a quick, spoiler-light reference to the character and their place in the saga.

Story Function

Dana helps define one of the central questions of the series: what happens when damaged, brilliant, ordinary, or overlooked people are placed inside a civilization-scale rescue attempt with tools powerful enough to change human destiny?
