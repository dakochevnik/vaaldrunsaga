War on Bureaucracy

Strategic Campaign

The effort to bypass or dismantle administrative systems that consume time, money, and human potential while pretending to protect the public.

Overview

This is a starter universe entry. It gives readers a stable landing page for the concept while leaving room for deeper canon, quotations, chapter links, and spoilers as the website matures.

Why It Matters

War on Bureaucracy is part of the larger VaalDrun question: can a failing civilization be repaired from the margins without simply reproducing the same mistakes under a better brand?
