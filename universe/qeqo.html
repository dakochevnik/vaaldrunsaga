QEQO

Guiding Principle

Question Everything. Question Often. QEQO is a habit of thought more than a slogan: a refusal to let inherited assumptions quietly steer civilization into failure.

Overview

This is a starter universe entry. It gives readers a stable landing page for the concept while leaving room for deeper canon, quotations, chapter links, and spoilers as the website matures.

Why It Matters

QEQO is part of the larger VaalDrun question: can a failing civilization be repaired from the margins without simply reproducing the same mistakes under a better brand?
