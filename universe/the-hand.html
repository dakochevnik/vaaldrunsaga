The Hand

Existential Unknown

The great uncertainty behind Opi’s calculations and Elias’s decisions: the possibility that forces beyond visible human politics are shaping the survival problem.

Overview

This is a starter universe entry. It gives readers a stable landing page for the concept while leaving room for deeper canon, quotations, chapter links, and spoilers as the website matures.

Why It Matters

The Hand is part of the larger VaalDrun question: can a failing civilization be repaired from the margins without simply reproducing the same mistakes under a better brand?
