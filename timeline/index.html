Timeline

A living timeline for The VaalDrun Saga. Dates and sequence details can be refined as the manuscript and website mature.

Before Humanity Knows

Opi monitors the long arc of human civilization and calculates the rising probability of collapse or extinction-level failure.

Stevens County First Contact

Opifera travels to northeastern Washington and confronts Elias Mercer during an extremely inconvenient moment on his morning run.

The Partnership Forms

Elias gives Opifera the nickname Opi, accepts the impossible faster than most humans would, and begins thinking of the crisis as a solvable systems problem.

Family Terms

Elias insists that his children and family must be protected before he fully commits to the mission.

Seattle Proof of Concept

Elias and Opi choose Seattle as an early test case for repairing homelessness, health, employment, and social trust at once.

The Parallel Emerges

What begins as intervention becomes structure: housing, purpose, responsibility, health, and community forming beside existing institutions.

The Husband Factory Realization

Elias and Opi realize their recovery system is turning damaged, excluded men into stable, capable husbands who otherwise would not have existed in the mating market.

The Wife Factory Realization

They extend the same logic globally: repair women who have also been excluded by trauma, poverty, illness, or disability, creating new families without raiding local mating pools.

Ambassadors of the Parallel

Restored people who remain in their own nations become natural ambassadors for future Parallel communities around the world.

Global Expansion

The Parallel begins shifting from an American experiment into a planetary civilization project.
